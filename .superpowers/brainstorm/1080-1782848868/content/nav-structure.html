How should the app be structured?

You want "today's workout" front and center. Which overall navigation model fits best?

A

Today-first, everything else is a drawer

App opens directly on today's workout. A hamburger or bottom tab gives access to the weekly schedule, history, and settings. Most sessions you never leave the workout screen.

B

Bottom nav with 3 tabs

Today (default) · Week (see all 5 days) · History (progress charts & logs). Clean, always-visible navigation. Familiar Android pattern.

C

Home dashboard → drill down

A small home screen shows the week at a glance (which days done, which is today) and you tap into today's workout. Good if you want to see the full week often.
